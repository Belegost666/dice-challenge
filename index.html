💖 Dice Challenge 💖

📜 GAME RULES ▼

1. The Dice Reserve

  • You begin your cycle with only 1 eight-sided die.
  • Every day after 06:00 AM, a new die is automatically added.
  • The more you wait, the more dice you get, increasing your chances.

2. The Objective

  • The sum of all your dice determines your winning chance (%).
  • Ex: 3 dice totaling 18 = 18% chance. 100+ = Guaranteed win.

3. The Draw

  • A gauge appears. If the cursor stops in the colored zone: Victory! Otherwise: Failure.

4. Consequences

  • Regardless of the result, you must masturbate for the given duration.
  • VICTORY: Allowed to cum at the end. Reserve resets.
  • FAILURE: Forbidden to cum. You keep your dice for tomorrow.

5. New Cycle

  • Reset at any time to start back with 1 die.
